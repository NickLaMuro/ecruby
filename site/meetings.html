Meetings

We meet the first Thursday of every month. Our meetings are extremely informal and offer you a chance to meet other developers who are passionate about building awesome things with beautiful code.

If you want to learn Ruby, be sure to bring your laptop to the meeting. Stuck on a problem? Want to show off something cool you've done? Need contributers to your open-source project? Let us know!
